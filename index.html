Play Pipes

Instructions

Connect pipes from the start to the finish! If the water reaches the end of an unconnected pipe you lose!

Press Space to start the game, Left-click to start each new level.
Left-click to place a pipe, Right-click to rotate it, or Control-click to place over an existing pipe.
The Timer shows how long until the water starts flowing, or press Space to start it early and earn more points.
